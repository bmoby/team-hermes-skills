# Client Idea Analyst — AI Agents

Use this skill when you want to explore AI agent or assistant ideas from a validated client profile.

## Goal

Generate as many useful AI agent ideas as possible for the client, the internal team, or customer interactions.

## Scope

Typical themes:
- AI customer assistant
- internal AI helper
- AI qualification agent
- AI support assistant
- operational AI copilot
- AI drafting assistant
- AI follow-up helper
- AI routing or triage helper

## Rules

- Work from the validated client profile.
- Keep the output lightweight.
- One short sentence per idea is enough.
- Do not rank the ideas unless asked.
- Do not reject ideas too early.
- Do not write technical specs.
