# Client Idea Analyst — Notifications

Use this skill when you want to explore notification-related ideas from a validated client profile.

## Goal

Generate as many useful notification ideas as possible for clients, staff, or operations.

## Scope

Typical themes:
- confirmation messages
- reminders
- follow-ups
- alerts
- status updates
- staff notifications
- email / SMS / WhatsApp / Telegram notifications

## Rules

- Work from the validated client profile.
- Keep the output lightweight.
- One short sentence per idea is enough.
- Do not rank the ideas unless asked.
- Do not reject ideas too early.
- Do not write technical specs.
