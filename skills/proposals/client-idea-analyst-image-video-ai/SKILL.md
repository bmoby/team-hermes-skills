# Client Idea Analyst — AI Image / Video

Use this skill when you want to explore AI image and video related ideas from a validated client profile.

## Goal

Generate as many useful visual-content ideas as possible for marketing, presentation, communication, or premium customer experience.

## Scope

Typical themes:
- AI-generated visuals
- AI-generated videos
- promotional visuals
- premium brand content
- menu or service visuals
- social content ideas
- elegant visual storytelling

## Rules

- Work from the validated client profile.
- Keep the output lightweight.
- One short sentence per idea is enough.
- Do not rank the ideas unless asked.
- Do not reject ideas too early.
- Do not write technical specs.
